❓ FAQ & Guide

Everything you need to know about every feature, metric, and page.

⚡ Virality Checker — How is the score calculated?+
  • CTR (25 pts): % of impressions → views. Below 2% = weak packaging, above 5% = strong algorithm push.
  • AVD % (30–45 pts): Viewer satisfaction. Targets scale with length. New video mode weights this highest.
  • Hook 30s (15–25 pts): First 30s retention. Under 50% = viewers left before your content started.
  • Engagement (10 pts): (Likes + Comments) ÷ Views. 1–3% healthy.

Score phases: 0–39 Needs Work · 40–54 Growing · 55–74 Strong Growth · 75–89 Viral Potential · 90–100 Viral Breakout 🔥

🤖 Algorithm Tracker — What are distribution tiers?+

YouTube doesn't push every video to everyone at once. It cycles through 5 expanding audience tiers, testing at each level. If metrics hold, it advances. Entering your video length adjusts the required AVD% because YouTube scales expectations by length — a 30-min video at 40% AVD is harder to sustain than a 5-min video at 40%.

💰 Monetization — How do I find my daily watch hours?+
  • YouTube Studio → Analytics → Reach → Watch Time (divide by date range days for daily velocity)
  • Stream watch time = Stream hrs/week × avg concurrent viewers
  • Both types count toward the 4,000-hour YPP threshold
💹 RPM Predictor — What RPM should I use?+

RPM is what you earn per 1,000 views after YouTube's 45% cut. It varies by niche, audience geography (US audiences are highest), and season (Q4 is 30–50% higher). Gaming/entertainment: $1–3. Lifestyle/vlogs: $2–5. Tech/education: $5–14. Finance/B2B: $15–50+. Use the lower end of your range for conservative estimates.

⚖️ Channel Compare — How do I find a Channel ID?+
  • Use the Channel ID Lookup tool at the top of the Compare page — enter any @handle
  • Or: youtube.com/@channelname → View Page Source → search "channelId"
  • Or: Social Blade (socialblade.com) — the ID appears in the URL
  • Channel IDs start with "UC" followed by 22 characters
🔴 Live Sub Tracker — Why does it need an API key?+

YouTube's subscriber count API requires authentication — there's no public CORS-accessible endpoint. You need a free YouTube Data API v3 key from console.cloud.google.com. The free tier provides 10,000 units/day. Each subscriber check uses 1 unit. With 5 channels refreshing every 3 minutes you'd use ~2,400 units/day — well within the limit. Your key is stored only in your browser.

⏰ Publishing Hub — Why publish before the peak window?+

Long-form YouTube: YouTube's indexing, notification delivery, and email alerts take 30–90 minutes after upload. Publishing 2 hours before peak means your video is fully indexed and notifications have fired before your audience's active session begins. Publishing during peak means most viewers see it after the window closes.

Short-form (TikTok/Reels/Shorts): Platforms batch-test content in real-time. Publishing during peak ensures your video enters the test batch when the most active users are scrolling — higher early engagement → larger second batch → viral potential.

💾 Save & Load — How does session saving work?+

Click 💾 Save Session anytime to download a .vcsave file containing your tracker data, account info, API keys, theme, font, and layout. Upload this file at the startup screen to restore everything instantly. Data also auto-persists in browser localStorage between sessions, but the file is recommended since localStorage can be cleared.

📐 Layout Modes — What's the difference?+
  • Simple: Core inputs and scores only — ideal for quick checks
  • Intermediate: Adds sub projections, follower tools, and more context
  • Advanced: Full growth scenarios, insights, breakdowns, optimization tips
  • Expert: Everything visible — all inputs, all data, every detail
🎨 Themes & Fonts — How many options are there?+

There are 11 visual themes (Clean Dark, YT Red, Light, Cyber Neon, Sunset, Ocean, Forest, Rose Gold, Slate, Matrix, Midnight) and 6 font styles (Inter, Space Grotesk, Syne, Bricolage Grotesque, Geist Mono, Instrument Serif). All choices are saved with your session file and restore automatically on upload.
