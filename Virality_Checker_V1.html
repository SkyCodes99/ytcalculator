📖 Understanding the Algorithm

🎯 Packaging (CTR)

CTR measures how well your topic and thumbnail convert browsing into viewing. If CTR is low, your content is invisible to potential new fans regardless of quality.

⏱️ Satisfaction (AVD)

AVD is the strongest signal of viewer satisfaction. High AVD tells YouTube to keep serving the video because it keeps users on the platform longer.

📈 Subscriber Growth Projection

Based on your virality score and views, estimate expected subscriber conversion rate. Industry average is 0.5–3% of total views converting to subscribers.

What is Sub Conversion Rate?
This is the percentage of total video views that result in a new subscriber. For every 1,000 views, a 1% rate = 10 new subs. This rate is influenced by: niche relevance (viewers staying in your topic), call-to-action strength (asking people to subscribe), content consistency (a clear brand or recurring series), and thumbnail/title alignment (viewers who feel the video matched expectations). New channels typically see 0.5–1.5%, established creators 1.5–3%, and viral outliers up to 5–10%.

Follower Growth Projection

TikTok follow rates from viral content vary by niche. An average FYP video converts 1–5% of unique viewers to followers.

What is Follow Conversion Rate?
This is the percentage of unique viewers that tap Follow after watching. Higher rates come from: strong niche consistency (viewers want more of the same), a compelling bio/profile that communicates your value, content that ends with an implied promise of more, and trending hooks that attract in-niche viewers rather than casual scrollers. Average creators see 1–2%; viral niche videos can hit 3–5%. Cross-promo and Stitch/Duet activity can push rates higher.

Follower Growth Projection

Instagram Reels convert approximately 0.5–3% of unique reach to followers when content hits the Explore page. High Save rates significantly improve this.

What is Follow Conversion Rate?
This is the percentage of people who see your Reel (reach) who then tap Follow. Conversion is driven by: your profile grid aesthetic (does it look intentional?), the relevance of your bio (clear value proposition), whether your previous Reels auto-play when they land on your profile, and how well your content fits a recognizable niche. Typical rates: 0.5–1% for general content, 1.5–3% for niche-consistent creators. Explore-page hits often spike this to 4–6% temporarily.
