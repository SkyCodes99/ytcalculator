Understanding the Algorithm

🎯 Packaging (CTR)

CTR measures how well your topic and thumbnail convert browsing into viewing. If CTR is low, your content is invisible to potential new fans regardless of quality.

⏱️ Satisfaction (AVD)

AVD is the strongest signal of viewer satisfaction. High AVD tells YouTube to keep serving the video because it keeps users on the platform longer.

TikTok Distribution Tiers

Initial Seed Batch (~200–500 Views)

Every video starts here. TikTok tests with a small seed group before deciding to push further.

Min. Completion:25%+
Min. 3s Hook:35%+
Niche Audience (~1K–10K Views)

Shown to interest-matched viewers. Engagement rate becomes the primary ranking signal.

Completion:35%+
Like Rate:3%+
Comment Rate:0.5%+
FYP Breakout (~10K–100K Views)

Broad FYP distribution. Share velocity becomes the key differentiator at this stage.

Completion:50%+
Share Rate:1.5%+
Viral / Mass Push (100K–1M+)

Algorithm validated at multiple levels. Content must be broadly accessible and highly shareable.

Completion:55%+
Share Rate:2.0%+
