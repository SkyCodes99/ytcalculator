💡 Potential New Features (Suggestions)

🔔 Upload Reminder Scheduler
Set recurring publish schedule reminders that fire as browser notifications at your optimal publish windows.
📝 Video Idea / Script Vault
A local note-taking vault for video ideas, outlines, and scripts. Organise by niche, status (idea/scripting/filming/editing), and priority.
🏷️ Title / Thumbnail A/B Tester
Log A/B test variants for titles and thumbnails with CTR results. Track which formats consistently win in your niche.
📦 Merch / Sponsorship Tracker
Track sponsorship deals, deliverables, CPM rates, and payment status alongside your content revenue.
🧮 Watch Hour Simulator
Input a video scenario (length, expected views, expected AVD%) and simulate how much it will contribute to your 4K goal before you publish.
📊 Cross-Platform Dashboard
Aggregate subscriber counts, total views, and follower counts across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram into one unified overview.
🎯 Niche Score Card
Input your niche, sub-niche, and channel specifics to receive a competition difficulty score, estimated RPM range, and growth difficulty rating.
📅 Content Calendar
Visual monthly calendar showing your scheduled publish days, past video entries, and upcoming ideas. Integrates with the Publishing Hub schedule.
📱 Shorts Strategy Planner
Dedicated tool for planning a Shorts-to-Long-Form funnel strategy — helping convert Shorts viewers to long-form subscribers.
🔍 Keyword & Tag Planner
Research and store optimal keyword clusters and tag strategies for your niche. Track which tag sets historically perform best.
